## 2. What Personal Data We Collect

We collect minimal personal information to provide our translation services. The data we may collect includes:

- **Account Information (if registered):** Email address, username, and password (encrypted)
- **Translation Content:** Text you submit for translation (processed temporarily and not permanently stored unless you save it to your account)
- **Usage Data:** Browser type, device type, IP address (anonymized), pages visited, and timestamps
- **Payment Information:** If you subscribe to premium features, payment details are handled securely through third-party payment processors (we never store full credit card information)

We **do not collect** sensitive personal data such as health information, political opinions, or biometric data.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

- **Provide Translation Services:** Process your text input and deliver accurate translations using AI technology
- **Account Management:** Create and maintain your user account, save your translation history (if you choose), and manage subscriptions
- **Service Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve our AI models, user interface, and overall service quality
- **Customer Support:** Respond to your inquiries, feedback, and support requests
- **Security & Fraud Prevention:** Protect our platform against misuse, abuse, and unauthorized access
- **Legal Compliance:** Comply with applicable laws and regulations

We **do not** use your data for targeted advertising or sell your information to third parties.

---

## 4. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

- **Translation Content:** Text submitted for translation is processed in real-time and deleted immediately after delivery of results, unless you save it to your account
- **Saved Translations:** Stored in your account until you delete them or close your account
- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days
- **Usage Analytics:** Anonymized and aggregated data may be retained for up to 2 years for statistical purposes
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data may be retained longer if required by law (e.g., payment records for tax compliance)

---

## 5. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR Article 6)

We process your data under the following legal bases:

- **Consent:** You provide explicit consent when creating an account or using our services
- **Contract Performance:** Processing is necessary to provide the translation services you requested
- **Legitimate Interest:** We process usage data to improve our services, ensure security, and maintain platform performance
- **Legal Obligation:** We may process data to comply with legal requirements such as tax regulations or law enforcement requests

---

## 6. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or other regions with data protection laws, you have the following rights:

- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Right to Rectification:** Ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):** Request deletion of your personal data
- **Right to Restriction:** Ask us to limit how we process your data
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing
- **Right to Lodge a Complaint:** File a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise your rights, please contact us at [hello@videtrans.com](mailto:hello@videtrans.com). We will respond within 30 days.

## 7. Automated Decision-Making and AI Processing

Our translation service uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to:

- Analyze linguistic patterns in your input text
- Detect source and target languages automatically
- Generate contextually accurate translations based on AI models
- Identify cultural nuances and idiomatic expressions

This automated processing **does not result in legal or significant effects** on you. It is used solely for providing translation services. You may review and edit any AI-generated translations before using them.

## 8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for basic site functionality (login sessions, preferences)
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand how users interact with our service (we use privacy-friendly analytics with IP anonymization)
- **Performance Cookies:** Improve loading times and service reliability

We **do not** use cookies for:

- Third-party advertising networks
- Cross-site behavioral tracking
- Selling data to advertisers

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may limit functionality.

VibeTrans is intended for users aged **13 and older**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at [hello@videtrans.com](mailto:hello@videtrans.com).

## 10. International Data Transfers

VibeTrans operates globally, and your data may be processed in countries outside the EEA. When transferring data internationally, we ensure:

- Data is processed only by providers with adequate data protection measures

- Encryption during transit and at rest for all sensitive data

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)

- **Regular Audits:** Periodic security assessments and penetration testing
